Final-Year Research Project · The Copperbelt University · 2026 – Present

Leading development of a Python-based primer design platform optimised for resource-constrained laboratory environments across Zambia and sub-Saharan Africa. Implements nearest-neighbour thermodynamics (SantaLucia 1998), automated hairpin ΔG and self-dimer detection, GC clamp evaluation, and homopolymer run checking.
